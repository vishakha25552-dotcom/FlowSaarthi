📄 1. PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

⸻

🧾 1.1 Overview

FlowSaarthi is an AI-powered platform that helps users:
	1.	Understand government documents in simple language
	2.	Navigate processes through step-by-step flows
	3.	Track progress visually via an interactive system

⸻

🎯 1.2 Objective

To reduce friction in Indian government interactions by transforming:
	•	confusion → clarity
	•	steps → structured flows
	•	processes → visual journeys

⸻

👤 1.3 Target Users

Primary
	•	Students & freshers
	•	Urban individuals handling documents themselves

Secondary
	•	Freelancers
	•	Small business owners
	•	Families managing paperwork

⸻

❗ 1.4 Problem Statement

Users struggle with:
	•	understanding official documents
	•	identifying next steps
	•	tracking progress across systems

This leads to:
	•	delays
	•	mistakes
	•	anxiety

⸻

💡 1.5 Core Value Proposition

“FlowSaarthi explains your situation and guides you step-by-step — visually and calmly.”

⸻

⸻

1. 🧾 Income Tax Notice
	•	Explanation of notice
	•	Deadline detection
	•	Response steps
	•	Required documents

⸻

2. 🪪 Aadhaar Update
	•	Address/mobile update flows

	•	Learner → DL journey

4. 📦 Refund / Consumer Complaint
	•	Issue explanation
	•	Complaint generation
	•	Escalation flow

⸻

5. 🧍‍♂️ Lost Documents Recovery
	•	Aadhaar / PAN / DL recovery
	•	Priority order
	•	Required actions

⸻

🔑 1.7 Core Features

⸻

🔹 1. Document Upload & Processing
	•	Upload:
	•	PDF / image
	•	Extract:
	•	raw text (OCR)
	•	key metadata

⸻

🔹 2. AI Explanation Engine
	•	Outputs:
	•	summary
	•	meaning
	•	urgency
	•	Tone:
	•	simple, human

⸻

🔹 3. Case Detection Engine
	•	Identifies:
	•	tax notice
	•	Aadhaar update
	•	etc.

⸻

🔹 4. Flow Generation Engine
	•	Converts case → structured steps
	•	Supports dynamic flow creation

⸻

🔹 5. Visual Flow Interface (CORE 🔥)
	•	Graph-based UI:
	•	nodes (steps)
	•	edges (transitions)

States:
	•	pending
	•	active
	•	completed
	•	blocked

⸻

🔹 6. Step Interaction Panel
	•	Instructions
	•	required documents
	•	action links
	•	“mark complete”

⸻

🔹 7. Progress Tracking
	•	Progress bar
	•	current step
	•	completion history

🎨 1.8 UX & Design Principles

⸻

🧠 UX Philosophy

Calm, guided, non-technical

⸻

🎨 Color System
	•	Deep Indigo → trust
	•	Soft Saffron → identity
	•	Muted Teal → progress
	•	Warm white → clarity

⸻

✨ Design Rules
	•	One action per screen
	•	Minimal text blocks
	•	No technical jargon
	•	Soft animations only
